Frequently Asked Questions

Can school admins see data from other schools?

No, school admins can only see their own school's data. Only you as Group Admin can see all schools.

How do I transfer students between schools?

Go to the student's profile, click "Transfer", and select the destination school. Their records move with them.

Can I limit which olympiads schools can register for?

Yes! Use the "Allowed Olympiads" setting for each school to control which competitions they can participate in.

How do group discounts work?

Discounts are calculated based on total registrations across all schools. More schools = higher discount tier!
