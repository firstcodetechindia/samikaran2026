This guide will help you understand how to use the Samikaran Olympiad platform. Whether you're registering for your first olympiad or checking your results, we've got you covered with simple step-by-step instructions.

How to Register for an Olympiad

1

Go to "Available Olympiads"

Click on the "Olympiads" tab in your dashboard. You'll see a list of all upcoming olympiads that you can participate in.

2

Choose Your Olympiad

Browse through subjects like Mathematics, Science, English, or Computer Science. Click on the olympiad you want to join.

3

Check Eligibility

Make sure the olympiad matches your class/grade level. Each olympiad shows minimum and maximum class requirements.

4

Complete Registration

Click "Register Now" and complete the payment (if required). You'll receive a confirmation message once registered.

💡 Pro Tip

Register early! Some olympiads have limited seats and early bird discounts. Check the registration deadline before the last date.

💻

How to Take an Exam

1

Check Your Exam Schedule

Go to "My Olympiads" to see when your exam starts. The exam window shows the dates during which you can attempt the exam.

2

Prepare Your Setup

Use a laptop or computer with a stable internet connection. Make sure your webcam works (required for proctored exams).

3

Start Your Exam

Click "Start Exam" when you're ready. Read all instructions carefully. The timer starts automatically once you begin.

4

Answer Questions

Read each question carefully. You can skip questions and come back later. Use the question navigator to track your progress.

5

Submit Your Exam

Click "Submit Exam" when finished. Review your answers before submitting. Once submitted, you cannot make changes.

⚠️ Important

During proctored exams, stay focused on the screen. Don't switch tabs, look away, or talk to anyone. The system monitors for any suspicious activity.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I lose internet during an exam?

Don't worry! Your answers are automatically saved. Just reconnect and continue from where you left off. The timer will show the remaining time.

Can I use a calculator during exams?

It depends on the exam. Some exams provide an on-screen calculator. Check the exam rules before starting.

When will I get my results?

Results are usually published within 7-14 days after the exam window closes. You'll receive a notification when results are ready.

How is negative marking calculated?

Some exams have negative marking where wrong answers reduce your score. Check the exam details to see if negative marking applies and how much.

Can I cancel my registration?

Yes, you can cancel before the registration deadline. Refund policies vary by olympiad. Check the terms before registering.

How do I use TARA AI assistant?

Click the TARA button on your dashboard. You can type questions or click the microphone to speak. TARA understands Hindi and English!

What is my Student ID?

Your Student ID (like SAM2512345) is shown on your dashboard and profile. Use it to login from any device instead of email.

Why was I logged out suddenly?

For security, you can only be logged in on one device. If you or someone logged into your account elsewhere, the previous session ends.
