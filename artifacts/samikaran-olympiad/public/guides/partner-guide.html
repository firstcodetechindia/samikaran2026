How to Earn Commissions

1

Get Your Unique Referral Link

Go to your dashboard and copy your unique referral link. This link tracks all registrations that come through you.

2

Share your link with schools, coaching centers, parents, and students. Use WhatsApp, email, or social media.

Watch your dashboard as students register through your link. See real-time registration counts and pending commissions.

Receive Weekly Payouts

Commissions are calculated weekly and transferred to your bank account. Minimum payout threshold is Rs. 500.

Frequently Asked Questions

When do I get paid?

Payouts are processed every Friday for the previous week's confirmed registrations. You need minimum Rs. 500 balance to receive payout.

What if a student gets a refund?

If a student cancels and gets a refund, the commission for that registration is deducted from your balance.

Can I have sub-partners under me?

Yes! Contact our partner team to learn about our multi-level partner program where you earn from your team's registrations too.

How do I promote to schools?

We provide school partnership kits with presentations, brochures, and sample letters. Download from your Marketing Materials section.
