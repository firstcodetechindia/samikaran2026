TARA AI Credit System

Meet TARA (Talent Assessment & Research Assistant)

TARA is the platform's AI-powered personal tutor. Students purchase credits to interact with TARA for homework help, concept explanations, and exam preparation.

Credit Pricing Model

TEXT Interactions (Word-Based Slab)

Response Length Credits Charged Example
1 - 100 words 2 credits Quick answers, definitions
101 - 300 words 4 credits Explanations, examples

500+ words 8 credits Comprehensive tutorials

VOICE Interactions - Samikaran Voice AI

Premium voice-enabled learning powered by Samikaran's proprietary Voice AI technology. Students can speak naturally and receive spoken responses from TARA.

// Samikaran Voice AI - Credit Calculation

Voice Credits = (Text + STT + TTS) × Voice Premium

// Components:
Text Credits = Word-based slab (2/4/6/8)
STT Credits = 1 credit (Samikaran Speech Recognition)
TTS Credits = Character Count × Rate
Voice Premium = 1.20 (20% premium for voice experience)

// TTS Rate (Admin Configurable)
Default: 1 credit per character
Range: 0.5 to 2.0 credits/char (adjustable)
