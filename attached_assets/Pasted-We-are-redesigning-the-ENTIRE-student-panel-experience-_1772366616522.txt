We are redesigning the ENTIRE student panel experience for Class 1–5 students.

CRITICAL:
- Do NOT modify backend logic.
- Do NOT change database schema.
- Do NOT edit ranking, XP, exam or MIRA logic.
- UI transformation only.
- All data must remain dynamic.

---------------------------------------

OBJECTIVE:
When a Class 1–5 student logs in, the entire portal must feel like a Little Champs Adventure World.

It must NOT feel like a SaaS dashboard.
It must NOT feel corporate.
It must feel playful, safe, rewarding, and joyful.

---------------------------------------

GLOBAL DESIGN SYSTEM (APPLY EVERYWHERE):

1. Background:
   - Use soft pastel gradients.
   - Add very subtle floating shapes (clouds, stars, blobs).
   - No dark sections.

2. Border Radius:
   - 20–24px for all cards.
   - Rounded pill buttons.

3. Shadows:
   - Soft floating shadows.
   - Slight card elevation.

4. Typography:
   - Friendly rounded font style.
   - Larger headings.
   - Less dense text.

5. Icons:
   - Cartoon style icons.
   - Colorful.
   - Not outline-only minimal icons.

---------------------------------------
