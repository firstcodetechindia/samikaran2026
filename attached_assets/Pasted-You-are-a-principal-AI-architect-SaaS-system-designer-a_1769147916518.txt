8. AI CONFIDENCE SCORING LOGIC (CRITICAL)
=====================================================

Calculate response confidence as:

confidence_score =
(knowledge_match * 0.5)
+ (flow_match * 0.3)
+ (ai_reasoning_score * 0.2)

RULES:
• knowledge_match = relevance score (0–1)
• flow_match = matched node accuracy (0–1)
• ai_reasoning_score = clarity & consistency (0–1)

MINIMUM CONFIDENCE REQUIRED = agent.confidence_threshold (default 0.75)

If confidence < threshold:
→ Trigger escalation flow
→ Do NOT answer directly
