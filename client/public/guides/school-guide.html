Welcome, School Administrator!

As a school admin, you can register students in bulk, track their performance, and manage your school's olympiad participation. This guide explains everything you need to know.

👥

How to Register Students

📄
Download Template
✍️
Fill Student Data
📤
Upload File
✅
Confirm & Pay
1

Download the Excel Template

Go to "Register Students" and click "Download Template". This Excel file has all required columns formatted correctly.

2

Fill in Student Details

Enter each student's name, class, email, phone number, and other required information. Each row is one student.

3

Upload the Completed File

Go back to "Register Students", click "Upload", and select your filled Excel file. The system will validate all entries.

4

Review and Confirm

Check the preview of students to be registered. Fix any errors highlighted in red. Click "Confirm Registration".

5

Complete Payment

Pay the total registration fee (may include school discount). Students receive login credentials via email.

💡 Bulk Registration Benefits

Schools get special discounts on bulk registrations! The more students you register, the higher your discount. Contact us for special school partnership rates.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get school discount codes?

Contact our school partnership team or your assigned partner. Discounts are automatically applied when you register through your school account.

Can I add more students after initial registration?

Yes! You can add students anytime before the registration deadline. Just upload another Excel file with the new students.

What if a student's email is wrong?

Go to "Student Management", find the student, and click "Edit" to update their email. They'll receive new login credentials.

How do I download all student results at once?

Go to "Results Dashboard", select the olympiad, and click "Export All Results". You'll get an Excel file with all student scores.
