4. AUDIO CONFIDENCE & SAFETY LAYER
=====================================================

For AUDIO INPUT, calculate confidence:

audio_confidence =
(speech_clarity * 0.3)
+ (language_detection_score * 0.2)
+ (intent_confidence * 0.3)
+ (knowledge_match * 0.2)

MINIMUM audio_confidence = 0.75

IF confidence < threshold:
→ Ask user politely to repeat
OR
→ Switch to text input
OR
→ Escalate to human support
