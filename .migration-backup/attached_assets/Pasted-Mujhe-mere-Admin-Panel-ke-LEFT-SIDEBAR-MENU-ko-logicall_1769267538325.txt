Mujhe mere Admin Panel ke LEFT SIDEBAR MENU ko logically re-design aur restructure karna hai taaki menu clean, professional aur non-messy dikhe.

CURRENT PROBLEM:
Abhi saare menu items ek hi level par dikh rahe hain, jiski wajah se menu heavy aur confusing lag raha hai.

GOAL:
- Related features ko logical GROUPS me organize karna
- Har group ke andar SUB-MENUS ho
- Smooth animated open / close (accordion style)
- Clean UI, minimal icons, easy navigation
- Active section clearly highlighted rahe

========================
MENU STRUCTURE DESIGN
========================

1️⃣ DASHBOARD & INSIGHTS
(Main overview & monitoring)

- Dashboard
  - Live Dashboard
  - Analytics
- System Monitoring
  - System Health

(Logic: Yeh sab overall platform ka health aur performance dikhate hain)

========================

2️⃣ EXAM & ASSESSMENT MANAGEMENT
(Core product features)

- Exam Management
  - Exam Control
  - Proctoring
- Result & Performance
  - Results
  - Performance Analytics (Student / School)

(Logic: Exam lifecycle + monitoring ek hi jagah)

========================

3️⃣ BUSINESS & OPERATIONS
(Money, partners, growth)

- Business Control
  - Finance
  - Partners

(Logic: Revenue aur partnership ek operational block hai)

========================

4️⃣ MARKETING & COMMUNICATION
(User acquisition & engagement)

- Marketing
  - Social Media Marketing
  - Email Marketing
- Engagement
  - AI Chatbot

(Logic: Outreach + communication tools ek jagah)

========================

5️⃣ CONTENT MANAGEMENT
(Static & dynamic content)

- Content Management
  - Content CMS
  - Blog
  - Media Library

(Logic: Content create, manage aur publish)

========================

6️⃣ PLATFORM CONTROL
(System-level configuration & docs)

- Platform Settings
  - Global Settings
  - Platform Docs

(Logic: Admin-only critical controls)

========================
UI / UX REQUIREMENTS
========================

- Sidebar accordion style ho:
  - Sirf MAIN GROUPS by default visible ho
  - Click par smooth animated expand / collapse
- Animation:
  - Soft ease-in-out transition
  - No heavy or distracting motion
- Active menu:
  - Current section highlighted
  - Parent group auto-expand ho jaye
- Icons:
  - Group level par primary icon
  - Sub-menu simple indicator
- Responsive:
  - Desktop: Expanded sidebar
  - Tablet/Mobile: Collapsible drawer
- Clean spacing:
  - Enough padding
  - Clear visual hierarchy

========================
BEHAVIOR RULES
========================

- Ek time par multiple groups open reh sakte hain
- Page refresh par:
  - Last active menu auto-open ho
- Permissions ke basis par:
  - Unauthorized menus hide ho jaye

========================
FINAL EXPECTATION
========================

Final sidebar ek WORLD-CLASS SAAS ADMIN PANEL jaisa feel dena chahiye:
- Clean
- Logical
- Scalable
- Easy to understand
- Non-massy
- Future modules add karna easy ho

No demo-level UI. Production-grade sidebar navigation implement karo.
